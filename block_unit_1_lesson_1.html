Lesson 1: What Are Blocks?

Welcome to Block Coding!

Blocks are colorful puzzle pieces that fit together to tell a computer what to do.

Drag & Drop

You can drag blocks and snap them together to make a program.

Blocks Have Meaning

Each block does something special, like moving, turning, or repeating actions.

Snap Blocks Together

When blocks snap together, the computer reads them in order and does what they say.
